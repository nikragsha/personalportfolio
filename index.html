Welcome to Personal Portfolio!


Personal Portfolio is a website that provides templates for personal portfolio websites.


There are twenty different background colors to choose from. Customizing a portfolio website requires you to only have a basic understanding of HTML and CSS.


Once you download a template from the website, customize it to your preferences. Feel free to customize and edit the site as you wish!


Visit the instructions section for instructions on how to get started - happy coding!
